Heads Up Display


This section will make you familiar with the Monkeystrike interface, the HUD (Heads Up Display).




  • 1. Weapon: This display shows which weapon you have currently selected, the number next to it stands for the weapon slot.


  • 2. Score: Here you can see how many points you have gained so far.


  • 3. Crosshair: The crosshair shows you where you are shooting at with your weapon and therefore helps you to aim.


  • 4. Health: This number displays the health points your monkey has left.


  • 5. Ammo: These numbers indicate how much ammo you have left in the current mag (left) and the amount of mags in total (right).


  • 6: Flybar: The flybar shows for how long you can continue flying. It is only visible to the Lemur, since he is the only class which has a backpack for flying, and it fills up again as soon as you stop flying.
