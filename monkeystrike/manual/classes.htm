Classes


Minimonkey

Attributes: Weapons:
Size: Small 1. Banana
Speed: Very Fast 2. Chainsaw
Armor: Weak 3. Akimbo Sabers
  4. Blowpipe

The Minimonkey is the only fictive monkey in Monkeystrike and represents all the small species.
No other class is as fast on the ground as the minimonkey, on the other hand he is only light armored.
His akimbo sabers are a dangerous melee weapon and with the blowpipe he can poison his enemies. A cute but deadly little monkey.



Lemur (Varecia variegata)

Attributes: Weapons:
Size: Small 1. Banana
Speed: Fast 2. Chainsaw
Armor: Weak 3. Coconut Launcher

The lemur is small and cannot take many hits. However he is the only class that has a backpack for flying. This makes the lemur very fast and hard to hit. The coconut launcher is a powerful weapon and it can only be used by this class so better don't underestimate him.



Chimpanzee (Pan troglodytes)

Attributes: Weapons:
Size: Average 1. Banana
Speed: Normal 2. Chainsaw
Armor: Medium 3. Strawberry Machinegun
  4. Banana Crossbow

The chimpanzee is basically an all-round class, all his attributes are pretty much average. He can fight at far range with his banana crossbow but can also attack on close distance with his strawberry machinegun. The chimpanzee's flexibility is probably his biggest advantage.



Baboon (Papio ursinus)

Attributes: Weapons:
Size: Average 1. Banana
Speed: Normal 2. Chainsaw
Armor: Medium 3. Strawberry Machinegun
  4. Grapefruit Shotgun


The baboon is a medium class and his attributes are equal to the ones of the chimpanzee. They show no big advantages or disadvantages. His grapefruit shotgun and strawberry machinegun make him an excellent warrior and very hard to hunt down in close combat. Don't let a baboon get too close to you or you will regret it.



Orang-Utan (Pongo pigmaeus)

Attributes: Weapons:
Size: Big 1. Banana
Speed: Slow 2. Chainsaw
Armor: Heavy 3. Pineapple Launcher
  4. Skunk


The orang-utan's attributes focus on heavy armor rather than speed. His pineapple launcher is a fearsome weapon, especially the homing mode makes it hard for enemies to escape and if the pineapples will not get you, the orang-utan's toxic-fart skunks probably will...



Gorilla (Gorilla gorilla)

Attributes: Weapons:
Size: Big 1. Banana
Speed: Slow 2. Chainsaw
Armor: Heavy 3. Melonpip Gatlinggun
  4. TNT Cat


The firepower of the gorilla is massive. He lets melonpips rain down on his opponents with the gatlinggun or sends explosive cats after them. The gorilla's armor can take/regularly takes quite some hits due to the slowness of the gorilla.
