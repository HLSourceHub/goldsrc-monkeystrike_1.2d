Gamemodes


In the first release of Monkeystrike you will be able to play 3 different gamemodes.
Check out the descriptions below for more information about them.


Deathmatch:

Monkey versus monkey. No teams, just pure deathmatch. Weapons come with the classes you pick before joining the match. The player with the most points wins.

Before joining the match, you will have to pick a class you would like to fight with. An image of the class, its weapons and attributes will appear when you hover above the class' name. Simply choose your class by clicking on the name. You can switch to another class at any point during the game, by pressing the "classmenu" button.




"Deathmatch" score system:

Frag - 10 points
Headshot - 20 points
Frags/Headshots count twice if the player is in bonustime ( bonustime icon ).
The prefix "ms_" before the map title indicates that it is a "Deathmatch" map.





Capture the Golden Banana:

This is a team based gamemode, therefore you will have to join a team before you can choose your class.

In "Capture the Golden Banana" and "Fruity Sacrifice" you will have to choose the team you would like to join before proceeding to the class menu. Click on the team's name for choosing or click on "Spectators" if you prefer to observe the match.




A golden banana is located in the middle of the map and both teams try to capture it in the hostile base. After capturing the banana or dropping it (by dying) it will respawn at its original location after a small delay.


"Capture the Golden Banana" score system:

Frag - 10
Headshot - 20
"Banana capture" - 100
The prefix "ctb_" before the map title indicates that it is a "Capture the Golden Banana" map.





Fruity Sacrifice:

This gamemode is also team based. In "Fruity Sacrifice" several fruits are spread over the level and both teams try to grab the fruits and bring them to their "sacrifice point" to score.
If a monkey gets killed he loses all the fruits he collected. The limit of fruits a player can carry is 3.
You can steal fruits from the opponent sacrifice point, so make sure that your fruits stay your fruits once you have brought them to your sacrifice point.


"Fruity Sacrifice" score system:

Frag - 10
Headshot - 20
Fruit capture - 0
Different from the other two gamemodes the team score cannot be raised by frags in "Fruity Sacrifice". The team score is representing the current number of fruits in your team's sacrifice point.


The prefix "fs_" before the map title indicates that it is a "Fruity Sacrifice" map.
