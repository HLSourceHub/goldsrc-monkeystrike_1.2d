Introduction


Welcome to the Monkeystrike manual. The manual is seperated into 9 chapters which cover all information you need for playing this modification.   In general, Monkeystrike is a multiplayer MOD for Half-Life in which the player can choose one of six different monkey classes which possess different attributes, weapons and features to compete with others in three gamemodes and in all kinds of cartoony environments. Feel free to browse through the chapters of this manual to gain more info about MS.
