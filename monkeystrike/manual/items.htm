Items




Bonustime item: This item is only available in Deathmatch. 3 spawn locations for the bonustime item can be found in every Deathmatch map, the item switches the location after getting picked up. The item enables a "bonus time" which makes you get twice the points for each frag.






Health item: The health item can be found in various locations in Deathmatch maps and most likely near the team spawn points in Fruity-Sacrifice and Capture the Golden Banana. It gives you 25 health points when you pick it up.






Ammo item: The ammo item gives you a small amount of ammo. Where you can find health items, you can often also find ammo items. Additionally, dead enemies drop this precious item.
